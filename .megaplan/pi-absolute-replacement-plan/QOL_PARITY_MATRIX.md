Date: 2026-07-04

Each row must be classified during Epic 1 as preserved, replaced, intentionally
dropped, or blocked. Broad facade adoption should not proceed while any
high-impact row is unknown.

## Acceptance Rule

Epic 1 must fill this matrix with exact current behavior and classify every row.
Epic 2 cannot expand beyond early adopters until high-impact rows have passing
acceptance tests or signed intentional differences.
