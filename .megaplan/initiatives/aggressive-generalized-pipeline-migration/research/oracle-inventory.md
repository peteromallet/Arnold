## Notes

- The `crash_isolation` and `version_skew` markers are registered in `pyproject.toml` but are **never applied as `@pytest.mark.crash_isolation` or `@pytest.mark.version_skew`**. Instead, those test files apply `hinge_gate` as a module-level `pytestmark`. A marker-expression gate would silently skip them.
- `tests/oracles/replay_oracle.py` is a helper module (not a test file) providing `assert_replay_parity`, `capture_legacy_action`, and `capture_routed_action`.
- `tests/oracle/fixtures/` contains golden traces used by `test_dual_run_oracle.py` and `test_m5d_substrate_swap.py`.
