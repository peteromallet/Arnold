**Permanently:**
- **`arnold_pipelines.megaplan`** — the canonical Megaplan product package. It houses `build_pipeline()` (returning explicit-node `workflow.Pipeline` data), policies, prompts, schemas, and receipts. M6 must NOT delete this; the plan's own M4 describes it as the product destination. The M6 deletion list confuses the product package with legacy bridge scaffolding inside it.

**Temporarily (during M4–M5 transition):**
- `_bridge.py`, `_compatibility.py`, `_forward_m2_m3.py` inside `arnold_pipelines/megaplan/` — scaffolding for repointing ~19 `run_pipeline` callers, subloop execution, resume paths, and demo entries. These are explicitly called out in `_BRIDGE_CALLERS.md` and the plan already flags them with "conformance TODOs that M6 will fail until removed." They must survive until every legacy caller is repointed.
