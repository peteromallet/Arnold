M11 Live Data Bridge Manual Smoke Checklist

These checks are info-only manual smoke coverage for physical browser devices. They are not required for CI and should not replace the jsdom integration tests.

Webcam

- Browser prompts for camera access from the live webcam canary.
- Granting access creates one live webcam source, opens a video frame channel, and updates the live-frame preview.
- Denying access reports a permission diagnostic and leaves no open channel or active media track.
- Removing or hot-reloading the extension stops all media tracks and leaves persisted timeline live binding metadata export-blocked until the user removes or bakes it.

MIDI

- A browser with Web MIDI support prompts through navigator.requestMIDIAccess.
- Granting access can register a controller source and deliver control samples without adding per-sample timeline history entries.
- Denying or disconnecting the device reports a diagnostic and leaves unresolved bindings blocked for export.

Serial

- A browser with Web Serial support prompts through navigator.serial.requestPort.
- Granting access can register a serial source and deliver data samples through a bounded live channel.
- Disconnecting the device reports a diagnostic and preserves unresolved binding metadata until explicit remove or bake.

Bluetooth

- A browser with Web Bluetooth support prompts through navigator.bluetooth.requestDevice.
- Granting access can register a Bluetooth source and deliver data/control samples through the provider-scoped registry.
- Cancelling the picker or losing the device reports a diagnostic and keeps export blocked for any unresolved persisted binding.

Generated Frames

- A generated-frame canary session shows explicit supersede or fork steering lineage before live delivery activates.
- Pending, refining, and final frame samples update preview progressively.
- Reject steering does not activate live delivery.
- Partial bake leaves unresolved ranges export-blocked; full bake produces deterministic asset or RenderMaterial refs that clear the live export blocker.
