**Acceptance:** each of the four interfaces exercised by BOTH backends with a real in-tree user; one non-planning oracle-`run(cmd)` caller uses oracle+dispatch+spine together; a hermes step through the service writes liveness on token-progress not silence (a4); two tenants over a shared key set observe one shared backoff AND one shared spend cap that stops the composition (a2/UU#8); a journaled `git push`/PR Effect replayed after a simulated crash-between-return-and-write does NOT re-execute (Effect Ledger oracle, UU#3); an Evaluand judgment round-trips through the one Ledger as a versioned join, not a float.
